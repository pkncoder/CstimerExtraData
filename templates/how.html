  1. Go to your Cstimer will all of your solves.
  2. Click on the export button in the top-left (looks like a little slab or table, with an arrow going out the top).
  3. In that menu click on export to file and open the file.
  4. WITHOUT MODIFING ANYTHING, copy everything by pressing control or command+a, then control or command+c
  5. Then go to this website and click on the Cstimer Extra Data tab, or tools.kiascottage.com/cstimerextras/extradata
  6. Click into the text box and paste (control or command+v) the data in. ONLY THE DATA.
  7. Finnaly your data will be shown.
  8. If you want to see the code behind this go to github.com/pkncoder/CstimerExtraData
